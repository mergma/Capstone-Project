What is MBTI?

The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) categorizes psychological types based on Carl Jung's theories, grouping them into 16 personality types using 4 dimensions.

While it’s widely used in workplaces and counseling, MBTI is often criticized for its lack of scientific rigor and is considered a pseudoscience.

MBTI Dimensions

Each MBTI type is a combination of 4 out of 8 traits:

Extraversion vs Introversion

Do you focus on the outer world or your inner world?

Sensing vs Intuition

Do you trust your senses or look for patterns?

Thinking vs Feeling

Do you decide with logic or emotion?

Judging vs Perceiving

Do you prefer structure or flexibility?
